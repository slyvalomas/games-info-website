top 10

the best 10 games of all time!

  1. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (2017) - An open-world action-adventure game that redefined the Zelda series.
  2. Red Dead Redemption 2 (2018) - An epic Western-themed action- adventure game known for its immersive storytelling and stunning graphics.
  3. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (2015) - A critically acclaimed action role-playing game with a vast open world and engaging narrative.
  4. God of War (2018) - A reboot of the God of War series, featuring a more mature Kratos and his journey with his son in the world of Norse mythology.
  5. Dark Souls III (2016) - An action RPG known for its challenging gameplay, intricate level design, and atmospheric world.
  6. Persona 5 (2016) - A Japanese role-playing game with a stylish aesthetic, engaging story, and unique social simulation elements.
  7. Uncharted 4: A Thief's End (2016) - An action-adventure game that serves as the conclusion to Nathan Drake's story, known for its cinematic presentation.
  8. Overwatch (2016) - A team-based multiplayer first-person shooter that emphasizes teamwork and unique hero abilities.
  9. Super Mario Odyssey (2017) - A platformer that takes Mario on a globe-trotting adventure with his sentient hat, introducing new gameplay mechanics.
  10. Horizon Zero Dawn (2017) - An action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world where robotic creatures roam, featuring a strong female protagonist.
